1. Validation = Proof People Want It

Most startups fail because founders guess. Validation gives real-world data.

2. Fastest way to validate a business

Create a simple landing page with:

  • What your product does
  • Who it helps
  • Benefits
  • A “Get Early Access” button

3. Get traffic to your landing page

  • Post in relevant FB groups
  • Post TikTok or Reels explaining the concept
  • Run a $5 ad test
  • Share in subreddits

4. If 20–50 people join your waitlist → it's validated
