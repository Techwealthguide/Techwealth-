1. What is Blockchain?

Blockchain is a distributed database that stores transactions publicly and cannot be changed. It is like a shared Google Doc that everyone can see but no one can edit retroactively.

2. Why it’s important

  • No central authority controls it
  • Transparent and secure
  • Backbone of Bitcoin, Ethereum, and DeFi

3. Proof of Work vs Proof of Stake

  • PoW: Bitcoin — miners solve puzzles
  • PoS: Ethereum — validators stake coins

4. Why this matters to beginners

Understanding blockchain helps you understand:

  • Why crypto has value
  • How wallets work
  • Why security is important
