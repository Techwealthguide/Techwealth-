Home Startup Home AI Tools

1. What makes an idea profitable?

A profitable idea solves a painful problem for a specific group of people.

Use this formula:

Market + Problem + Solution = Business

2. The 5 Fastest Places to Find Ideas

  • Reddit communities
  • Facebook Groups
  • TikTok comments
  • Competitor reviews (Amazon, G2, AppStore)
  • AI tools directories

3. Types of profitable online businesses

  • AI automation services
  • Digital products (courses, templates)
  • Simple SaaS apps
  • Consulting services
  • E-commerce (dropshipping / print-on-demand)

4. The 3 Golden Rules

  • Start small, validate fast
  • Sell first, build later
  • Use AI to save time & cost
