Wallets

Crypto wallets store your private keys. Two types:

  • Hot Wallets: MetaMask, Trust Wallet
  • Cold Wallets: Ledger, Trezor (offline & safest)

Exchanges

Exchanges like Binance allow you to:

  • Buy crypto with card or bank transfer
  • Trade coins
  • Stake, earn rewards, launchpad
