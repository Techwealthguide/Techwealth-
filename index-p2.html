Top Hosting for Tech Startups

Reliable hosting providers for AI, apps, and tech projects.

  • AWS: Highly scalable, best for production-grade infra. (Good for heavy compute; complex to manage.)
  • Google Cloud: Great for ML integrations and Google APIs.
  • Azure: Enterprise features and ML services.
  • DigitalOcean: Simple, cost-effective droplets for smaller projects and prototypes.
  • Vercel / Netlify: Best for frontend and Jamstack apps (fast deployments).

Tip: use managed ML services (e.g., Vertex AI, SageMaker) for heavy model training and inference to reduce ops burden.
