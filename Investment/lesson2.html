The 4 key things to check before buying ANY tech stock

1. Revenue growth

Is the company making more money each year? Tech companies must show momentum.

2. Competitive advantage

Does the company have something others can’t easily copy? Examples:

  • Apple: ecosystem
  • NVIDIA: AI chips
  • Microsoft: enterprise dominance

3. Management quality

Strong CEOs = strong companies. Examples:

  • Elon Musk (Tesla, SpaceX)
  • Jensen Huang (NVIDIA)
  • Satya Nadella (Microsoft)

4. Debt vs cash

A good tech company has:

  • Low debt
  • Strong cash reserves
