1. What is the stock market?

The stock market is simply a platform where you can buy ownership in companies. When you buy a stock, you own a piece of the business.

2. Why companies sell shares

They raise money to:

  • Build products
  • Expand operations
  • Hire staff

3. Why YOU can profit

Because when a good company grows, the value of your shares increases. Plus, some companies pay dividends (passive income).

4. Long-term vs short-term investing

  • Long-term: Buy & hold great companies for years (best for beginners)
  • Short-term: Trading — higher risk, requires skills
